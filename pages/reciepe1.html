Chocolate Peppermint Bark

A simple yet elegant treat combining rich dark chocolate with a refreshing hint of peppermint.

Ingredients

  1. 1. 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  2. 2. cup white chocolate chips
  3. 3. 1/2 tsp peppermint extract
  4. 4. Crushed candy canes for topping

Steps

  • ➡️Melt the dark chocolate and spread it evenly on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • ➡️Let it set, then melt the white chocolate and spread it on top.
  • ➡️Add peppermint extract to the white chocolate before spreading.
  • ➡️Sprinkle crushed candy canes over the white chocolate layer.
  • ➡️Chill until firm, then break into pieces.
