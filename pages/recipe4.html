Decadent Chocolate Truffle Balls

Smooth, melt-in-your-mouth chocolate truffles rolled in festive toppings.

Ingredients

  1. 1. 8 oz bittersweet chocolate, finely chopped
  2. 2.1/2 cup heavy cream
  3. 3. Cocoa powder, sprinkles, or crushed nuts for coating

Steps

  • ➡️ Heat heavy cream and pour it over chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth.
  • ➡️ Chill the mixture for 2 hours or until firm.
  • ➡️ Scoop small amounts of the ganache and roll them into balls.
  • ➡️ Roll the truffles in your desired coating.
