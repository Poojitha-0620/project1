Chocolate-Dipped Strawberries for Any Occasion

Smooth, melt-in-your-mouth chocolate truffles rolled in festive toppings.

Ingredients

  1. 1.1 lb fresh strawberries (washed and thoroughly dried)
  2. 2. 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate (chopped or chips)
  3. 3. 4 oz white chocolate (optional, for decoration)
  4. 4. Optional toppings:--Crushed nuts,Sprinkles,Shredded coconut

Steps

  • ➡️ Rinse the strawberries gently and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  • ➡️ Ensure they are completely dry to avoid the chocolate seizing.
  • ➡️ Melt the Chocolate.
  • ➡️ Hold each strawberry by the stem and dip it into the melted chocolate.
  • ➡️Twirl gently to coat it evenly, letting the excess drip off.
  • ➡️Place the dipped strawberries on a baking sheet lined with parchment or wax paper.
  • ➡️Allow them to set at room temperature or in the refrigerator for 15–20 minutes.
  • ➡️Serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 48 hours.
