Chocolate-Dipped Shortbread Cookies

Buttery shortbread cookies dipped in rich chocolate and decorated with sprinkles.

Ingredients

  1. 1.1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  2. 2.1/2 cup sugar
  3. 3. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  4. 4.1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    6. 5.1 cup melted dark chocolate

Steps

  • ➡️ Cream butter and sugar together, then add vanilla extract. Mix in flour to form a dough.
  • ➡️ Roll out the dough and cut into desired shapes. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 12–15 minutes.
  • ➡️ Once cooled, dip the cookies halfway into melted chocolate and let them set on parchment paper.
