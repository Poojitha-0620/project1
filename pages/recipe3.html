Hot Chocolate on a Stick

Perfect for gifting, these adorable chocolate sticks transform into a creamy hot chocolate when stirred into warm milk.

Ingredients

  1. 1. 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  2. 2.1/2 cup heavy cream
  3. 3. Mini marshmallows (optional)
  4. 4. Wooden sticks or skewers

Steps

  • ➡️ Heat heavy cream until just simmering, then pour over chocolate chips. Stir until smooth.
  • ➡️ Pour the mixture into small silicone molds and insert wooden sticks.
  • ➡️Add marshmallows on top if desired. Let set completely.
  • ➡️Package in cellophane bags for gifting or store in the fridge.
