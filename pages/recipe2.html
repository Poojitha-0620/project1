Gooey Chocolate Lava Cake

A restaurant-worthy dessert with a molten chocolate center that oozes perfection.

Ingredients

  1. 1. 4 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  2. 2.1/2 cup unsalted butter
  3. 3. 1 cup powdered sugar
  4. 4. 2 large eggs + 2 egg yolks
  5. 5. 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  • ➡️Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease ramekins and dust with cocoa powder.
  • ➡️Melt chocolate and butter together. Stir until smooth.
  • ➡️Whisk in powdered sugar, eggs, and yolks. Add flour and mix until combined.
  • ➡️Pour batter into ramekins and bake for 12 minutes.
  • ➡️Serve immediately, dusted with powdered sugar and a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
